## Common Pitfalls to Avoid

### ❌ Don't Do This
1. **Pilot without metrics:** You must measure before/after to prove value
2. **Tool sprawl:** Don't let every engineer pick their own tools—standardize
3. **Skipping training:** AI tools require learning—dedicate time
4. **Ignoring skeptics:** Engage resistors early, address concerns transparently
5. **Over-relying on AI:** Always validate AI output, especially for security/critical systems
6. **Under-communicating:** Share progress weekly—keep excitement high

### ✅ Do This Instead
1. **Measure everything:** DORA metrics, time saved, satisfaction
2. **Celebrate wins:** Share success stories every week
3. **Iterate quickly:** If something isn't working, adjust within days, not months
4. **Involve everyone:** Even if not in pilot, keep full team informed
5. **Document learnings:** Build knowledge base as you go
6. **Think long-term:** 90 days is just the start—plan for multi-year journey
